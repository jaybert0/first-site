Coaching

"Anyone can make someone TIRED. Not everyone can make someone BETTER." - Martin Rooney

I have grown a lot in my 14 years in the Fitness Industry. After starting off as wanna-be meathead trainer only concerned about getting bigger and benching more I grew into to a coach whose priority is to feel better first, move better second, and look better last, all based on the goals, values and priorities of who I'm coaching.


There are 168 hours in a week. At your best you will spend 6-8 hours working out. That leaves 160 hours to either support or mitigate the work you do in the gym. My coaching is about maximizing your investment, both time and money, while living your life.

My objective is to take as much or as little of the guess work out of what you "should" be doing both inside and outside the gym.
